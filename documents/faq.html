Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be returned in their original condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days. Customs duties may apply depending on your country.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the carrier's site.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and UPI (India).
